TERMS & POLICIES

  1. Applicants can register only once in the admission portal for this institution. On successful completion of registration a unique 10 digit application number is generated, which the applicant receives in SMS and Email if provided, along with his/her chosen password.
  2. The system checks whether an applicant is unique from the following data – Name, Roll No. (10 + 2) , Mobile No. and e-mailid
  3. Applicant is responsible to provide correct information/data, to the best of his/her knowledge, while filling up the online form and in the process if it is found that there is any discrepancy, then his/her application is liable to be cancelled.
  4. After confirmation any candidate will not able to modify any information
  5. After confirmation of the registration the candidate can take a printout of the Application Form. Please note that after publication of provisional merit list no modification of data is allowed at the applicant end.
  6. It is mandatory/compulsory for a candidate to provide his/her personal mobile number and emailid which may be used to send various information during the process of admission.
  7. To apply for new subject/s after confirming the application, a candidate needs to login using his/her registration ID and password and click the link ‘apply more subjects’ and add new subject to the application.
  8. The verification of all the documents according to the declaration in the form submitted will be done only on the date of commencement of classes.
  9. Admission will be cancelled if the documents are found not in conformity with the declaration in the forms submitted.
